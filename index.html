About me

Since May 2024 I am an associate professor in the Algebra Group at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of UiT, the Artic University of Norway.

Before joining UiT, I held positions as reserach group leader at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences, as substitute professor at Universität Osanbrück, and as a postdoc at TU Berlin in the research groups Discrete Mathematics/Geometry and Nonlinear Algebra.

I obtained my PhD in October 2016 at KU Leuven under the supervision of Filip Cools, Marc Coppens and Wim Veys.

Research interests

My research domains are algebraic geometry, tropical geometry and their interactions, with a focus on computational aspects. I have been working on topics involving geometric and combinatorial properties of tropical curves and surfaces, matroids and tropical linear spaces, realization spaces of polytopes, lattice polytopes and their triangulations.
